01 · Sales

New and used vehicle sales.

We source and supply cars and light commercial vehicles to order. Whether you're after a specific make and model or open to recommendations within a budget, we handle the search, inspection, paperwork and handover.

New Vehicles

Factory-order and pre-registered vehicles from major manufacturers. Full manufacturer warranty and roadside assistance included as standard.

Used Vehicles

Independently inspected used cars from trusted trade sources. Every vehicle is HPI-checked and comes with a written history report.

02 · Leasing

Personal, business and PCO leasing.

Contract hire and PCO rental arrangements structured around how you use the vehicle. Clear monthly figures, defined terms, no surprises.

Personal Contract Hire

Fixed monthly payments over an agreed term and mileage. Road tax and manufacturer warranty included; maintenance packages available on request.

Business Contract Hire

Tax-efficient vehicle arrangements for limited companies, sole traders and fleets. VAT-reclaimable on qualifying vehicles.

PCO Rental

TfL-licensed vehicles for private hire drivers on weekly or monthly terms. Insurance, servicing and MOT included in the weekly figure.

03 · Servicing

Scheduled servicing, MOT and diagnostics.

Manufacturer-standard servicing by qualified technicians, using genuine or matching-quality parts. Full service history maintained and provided in writing.

Full & Interim Service

Comprehensive vehicle inspection, fluid changes and consumables replacement in line with manufacturer schedules.

MOT Testing

Class 4 MOT testing with pre-test checks. If your vehicle needs work to pass, we'll quote clearly before proceeding.

Diagnostics

Dealer-level diagnostic equipment for identifying warning lights, electrical faults and emissions issues.

04 · Repairs

Mechanical, electrical and bodywork repairs.

From brakes and clutches to accident repair and paintwork. Written quotes before any work is authorised, and a workmanship guarantee on completion.

Mechanical

Engine, transmission, suspension, brakes and clutch work. Same-day repairs on common jobs where parts are available.

Electrical & ECU

Battery, alternator, starter motor and wiring faults. ECU coding and key programming for most makes.

Bodywork & Paint

Panel repair, dent removal, resprays and insurance-approved accident repair. Colour-matched to manufacturer specification.

About

A straightforward motor trade business.

Black Label Automotive is a London-based motor trade business offering vehicle sales, leasing, servicing and repairs under one roof. We work with private buyers, business customers and PCO drivers, and we aim to keep the process clear: fixed quotes, plain-English contracts, and a single point of contact for the life of your vehicle.
